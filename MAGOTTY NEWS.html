5 schools book second-round berths

ANCHOVY, St James — Former champions Cornwall College and Garvey Maceo High were among five schools who booked second-round spots in the ISSA/Wata daCosta Cup football competition with wins in yesterday's first round return leg games played on a rain-affected day. In addition to advancing Cornwall College, Pt Antonio High and Garvey Maceo and St Elizabeth Technical High School (STETHS), who had advanced in the previous set of games, all won their respective zones, while Cedric Titus and Manchester High also booked spots in the next round. At least six games were not played due to heavy rains or waterlogged fields and are expected to be played next week.
